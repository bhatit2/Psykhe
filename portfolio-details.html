Relationship counselling

As a relationship counsellor we provide support and guidance to couples or individuals facing challenges in their relationships.

Talk to the therapist about ..

Communication problems

If you are often struggling with effective communication, leading to misunderstandings, arguments, and feelings of distance. Our therapists can help couples with better communication techniques and help them express their needs and concerns more effectively.

Conflict resolution

Disagreements and conflicts are inevitable in relationships. Counselling can assist couples in resolving conflicts in a healthy and constructive manner, helping them find mutually agreeable solutions and avoid destructive patterns.

Intimacy and sexual issues

Intimacy issues are not easy to communicate. It is common for people to hide intimacy-related problems as they expect things to get worse. Unfortunately, holding back such emotions extrapolates the problem further. Intimacy Counselling gives the couple a safe space to communicate their unmet needs which can range from sex, romance, meaningful dialogue, finances, spiritual connect & other aspects which may limit a deeper connection.

Trust and infidelity

Rebuilding trust after infidelity can be challenging. Cheating in a relationship is a devastating and emotionally painful experience. Many couples part ways and marriages get torn apart due to infidelity. While cheating can be considered a relationship-ending event, building a much more meaningful and intimate relationship post is possible. Counselling can guide couples through the healing process, helping them rebuild trust, address underlying issues, and develop strategies to prevent future infidelity.

Life transitions and adjustments

Significant life changes such as marriage, having children, or career shifts can strain relationships. Counselling can support couples in navigating these transitions, managing stress, and adapting to new circumstances.

Pre-marital counselling

Couples considering marriage often seek pre-marital counselling to address potential challenges and build a strong foundation for their relationship. Counselling can help couples explore topics such as expectations, values, communication, and conflict resolution before entering into marriage.

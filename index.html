About Me

Meet Divya Manani, your wellness & mindfulness coach.

With a deep passion for helping others,Divya is a highly qualified and empathetic psychologist dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalised care. She has 6+ years of experience and a proven track record of empowering clients to overcome obstacles and thrive in their personal and professional lives.

Approachable and compassionate, Divya understands that each person's journey is unique. She creates a warm and non-judgmental space where you can feel comfortable opening up about your thoughts, emotions, and concerns. Through active listening, genuine empathy, and an unwavering commitment to your well-being, Divya will work closely with you to understand your individual needs and collaboratively develop a customised healing plan.

What sets Psykhe apart?

Holistic Approach

Psykhe believes in treating the whole person, considering the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit. Drawing from a range of therapeutic modalities, including cognitive-behavioural therapy (CBT), psychodynamic therapy, and mindfulness practices, [he/she] tailors the treatment approach to address your unique circumstances and goals.

Strength-Based Focus

Psykhe recognizes that you possess inner strengths and resources that can be harnessed to navigate life's challenges. This will help you identify and build upon these strengths, empowering you to overcome obstacles, improve self-esteem, and cultivate resilience.

Collaborative Partnership

Your therapeutic journey is a collaborative effort. We will actively engage with you, seeking your input and working together to explore underlying issues, set achievable goals, and develop effective strategies for personal growth. You will be supported and encouraged every step of the way.

Safe and Confidential Environment

Confidentiality is of utmost importance in the therapeutic relationship. Psykhe adheres to the highest ethical standards to ensure your privacy and confidentiality. You can trust that your personal information and sessions will remain strictly confidential.
